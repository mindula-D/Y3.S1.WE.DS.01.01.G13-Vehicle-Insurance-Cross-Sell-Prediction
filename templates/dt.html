What is Decision Tree?

Think of a decision tree like a flowchart that helps us make choices. In the context of vehicle insurance, it's like a series of questions that lead to a decision. For example, it might start with a question like, "Is the driver under 25 years old?" If the answer is yes, it might lead to a decision that the insurance is more expensive. Decision trees help the system make predictions based on a set of rules and information.
